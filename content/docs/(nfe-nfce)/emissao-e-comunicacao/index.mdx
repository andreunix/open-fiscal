```mermaid
flowchart LR
    X[XML UTF-8] --> S[Assinatura ICP-Brasil]
    S --> T[TLS + SOAP 1.2]
    T --> W[Web Service da autorizadora]
    W --> R[Recibo / Protocolo]
```
